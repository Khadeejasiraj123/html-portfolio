The Best Movies According To Khadeeja

My Top 3 Movies Of All-Time

The Fault in Our Stars

Hazel Grace Lancaster, a cancer patient, meets and falls in love with Augustus Waters, a fellow survivor. Their love story is a touching exploration of life, love, and loss amid illness.

Red, White & Royal Blue

The First Son of the U.S., Alex Claremont-Diaz, and Prince Henry of Wales turn their rivalry into a secret romance, challenging political and personal boundaries.

Me Before You

Louisa Clark becomes a caregiver for paralyzed banker Will Traynor. As their bond deepens, they face life-changing decisions in this exploration of love, loss, and life's value.
